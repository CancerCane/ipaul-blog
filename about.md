I'm a retired veteran navigating the next chapter — one where the mission is personal and the learning never stops.

This blog is where I document my tech journey, share takes on what's happening in the world, and write about life after service. Some posts will be tutorials I wish I'd had. Others will be opinions you didn't ask for. All of them will be honest.

## What you'll find here

- **Tech** — Learning new tools, building projects, breaking things, fixing them
- **Opinion** — World news through the lens of someone who's seen a few things
- **Life** — The retirement chapter nobody prepares you for

## The backstory

I built [iPaul Media](https://ipaul.us) as a veteran-owned creative studio in Dallas. That work taught me web development, design, and how to ship things that matter. This blog is the personal side of that journey.
