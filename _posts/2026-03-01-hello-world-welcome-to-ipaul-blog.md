This is it — the first post.

I've been building websites for others through [iPaul Media](https://ipaul.us) for a while now. But I never really made a space to write for myself. This blog changes that.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about three things:

1. **Tech** — I'm constantly picking up new tools and frameworks. Some of it sticks. Some of it doesn't. I'll document both.
2. **Opinions** — The world keeps spinning and I keep having thoughts about it. Might as well put them somewhere.
3. **Life** — Retirement isn't what I expected. It's better in some ways, harder in others.

## Why now?

Honestly? Because I realized I've been consuming content for years without contributing much back. The tech community gave me a second career after the military. Writing is how I give back.

> "The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now."

So here we are. Welcome aboard.
